In this programming project, you are going to implement the advanced shortest paths algorithms from the lectures, apply them to the real-world road networks and social networks graphs and optionally compete against each other on the forums based on whose implementation is the fastest. In the end, you will also solve the classical logistics problem: find the optimal way to leave the depot, visit a list of stores to deliver the goods, and return to the depot. It is known as Travelling Salesman Problem, and your programs will be able to schedule the logistics on the real road map graphs.
